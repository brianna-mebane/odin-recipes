Classic S'mores

Description

It's hard to write a proper introduction for s'mores given how iconic the dessert is. The perfect sweet treats for anyone camping outdoors around an open fire (or glamping from the comfort of their own home!), s'mores are a simple yet inviting recipe elementary enough for even the biggest culinary novices to complete, courtesy of Allrecipes.

Ingredients

  • One (1) large marshmallow
  • One (1) large graham cracker
  • One (1) chocolate bar of your choice (milk, dark, etc.)

Steps

  1. Gather all of your ingredients.
  2. Heat your marshmallow over an open flame until it starts to brown and melt in texture.
  3. Break your graham cracker in half and sandwich a piece of your chocolate bar between one piece of graham cracker and the now-heated marshmallow. Place another piece of graham cracker on top of the marshmallow.
  4. Allow the s'mores sandwich to cool down before consuming.
